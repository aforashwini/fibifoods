About Us

We're on a mission to make fiber the most delicious part of your day.

Fibi was born out of frustration. As a health-obsessed vegan who tracks fiber like others track protein, our founder couldn't find a single convenient fiber source that didn't taste like cardboard, feel like medicine, or come wrapped in a list of ingredients that read like a chemistry textbook.

Here's the truth: 95% of Americans don't get enough fiber. That's not a minor gap — fiber is the unsung hero of gut health, heart health, blood sugar control, and even mood regulation. Yet the products on shelves treat it like an afterthought — something to choke down, not something to enjoy.

We called in the expert. Fibi bars were co-developed with a recipe developer and food blogger with 25+ years of culinary experience — someone with a reputation for turning healthy ingredients into legendary dishes in her Bangalore kitchen.

The result? A recipe that balances high-impact nutrition with culinary-grade flavor. Every bar is hand-crafted with whole foods you can see, taste, and trust.

Our Story

01

The Problem

We looked at every fiber bar, supplement, and powder on the market. They were either loaded with artificial sweeteners and isolated fibers, or they tasted like sawdust wrapped in false promises. The fiber aisle was broken — and nobody seemed to care enough to fix it.

02

The Obsession

Our founder spent over a year obsessing over recipes. Hundreds of test batches. Dozens of ingredient combinations. Feedback from family, friends, nutritionists, and anyone who would take a bite. The goal was simple: make something that someone who doesn't care about fiber would still devour.

03

The Breakthrough

When we partnered with a legendary recipe developer from Bangalore — a culinary artist who'd spent 25+ years making healthy food taste extraordinary — everything clicked. She brought traditional food wisdom to modern nutrition science, and the Fibi bar was born.

04

The Mission

Today, Fibi is more than a bar. It's a movement. We believe fiber shouldn't be a chore — it should be the snack you reach for because it's genuinely delicious. We're building a future where high-fiber eating is the norm, not the exception. One bar at a time.
