The Backstory

Mom-Tested,
Founder-Obsessed.

Fibi was born out of frustration. As a health-obsessed vegan who tracks fiber like others track protein, our founder couldn't find a convenient fiber source that didn't taste medicinal.

So, we called in the expert. Fibi bars were co-developed with a recipe developer and food blogger with 25+ years of culinary experience (and a reputation for legendary healthy cooking in Bangalore).

The result? A recipe that balances high-impact nutrition with culinary-grade flavor.
